"NATIONAL SYMBOLS OF INDIA"

NATIONAL FLAG

This is national flag of India.
It has three colour saffron,white and green.
It is also called TIRANGA.

NATIONAL EMBLEM

The national emblem has been adopted from the lion capital of Ashoka at Sarnath.
It consists of four lions standing back to back.

NATIONAL ANTHEM

JANA GANA MANA is our national Antheme.
It is written by RABINDRA NATH TAGOR.

LOTUS IS OUR NATIONAL FLOWER

NATIONAL BIRD

PEACOCK is our national bird of India.
